Team Five

We are students in Western Michigan University and for our Final and Midterm project, we chose to make a fictional city website "Springfield" in the world of The Simpsons which contains a page that shows people descriptions and images of places that one may visit if they were somehow able to find the fictional city such as Lard Lad Donuts, Krusty Burger, Krusty Land, Duff Brewery Beer Garden etc. We also made a store page that sells t-shirts, merhcandise of well known people around Springfield, and a well known couch. We also made a page that tells the brief history of how Springfield was founded. There are also a couple of geographic landmarks of the city on the History page. We also included a section on the culture of Springfield to show the diversity of the city. Near the bottom of the page contains a table which shows the crime rate of the city.
