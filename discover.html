Springfield is a city in Springfield County and is located within the continental United States. It is located near the cities of Capital City, Ogdenville, and North Haverbrook. The city is a sister city to Eugene, Oregon. There are many different restaurants and attractions in Springfield such as Krusty Burger which is a fast food restaurant chain owned by Krusty the Clown as one of his many branded products and services, Kwik-E-Mart which is a convenience store located in Springfield and run by Apu Nahasapeemapetilon, Lard Lad Donuts which is a donut store that has an (estimated) 8-metre (26 ft) tall statue of a rather chubby boy proudly holding a donut over his head as thier mascot, Moe's Tavern which is a local bar in Springfield run by Moe Szyslak and many more! There is even an amusement park called Krustyland.
