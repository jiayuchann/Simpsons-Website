Big mountains, deserts, forests, lakes, volcanos, everything you can imagine, you can find them here. With a population of about 60,000, and located right next to Shelbyville, it has been dubbed "America's Worst City".

The city has witnessed 2 American civil wars as well as being the target of many Native American raids, before it was founded by Jebediah Springfield in 1796.

Jebediah was seen as the hero of the town. There were even stories about him killing a bear with only his fists.


How Springfield and ShelbyVille became neighbouring rivals


Jebediah's group left after he refused to found a town where men can have the right to marry their cousins. Til this day, there's still a strong rivalry between the 2 cities
